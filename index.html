Project Ideas

Overview

My project idea is to create a free and easy-to-use VPN (Virtual Private Network). Users need to install a software and have a crypto currency wallet address to use it. Everyone who wants to use it for free needs to receive Ads and also make his/her own device a VPN server for others to connect. By doing this , they can also receive a kind of crypto currency called VPNC (Virtual-Private-Network Coin). Users can always pay fiats or VPNC to remove ads and stop sharing with others. There will be a small discount if users choose to pay VPNC. Users can get VPNC by sharing their devices or buying it on exchanges. The VPN servers are decentralised, private and secure.

Motivation

Nowadays, World Wide Web plays an important role in almost every part of life. It is never been so easy and convinent to get information from other countries or communicate with people overseas. However, in some authoritarian states, the government see the Internet as a threat to their absolute control of power and people's thoughts.

Description

People who wish to download can send a email with crypto currency wallet address to the email address which is owned by the the project company. A VPNC with download link of the software will be automatically sent to the crypto currency wallet address. This may naturalise Internet filtering from authoritarian government as users no longer need to go to a certain website to get a download address. Besides, nether the wallet nor the coin can be deleted by anyone. With smart contract, this coin can not be sent to another address by users and will be automatically sent back in a period of time. Thus the company can have enough coins to send to new users.Once downloaded, users can choose a VPN address and connect. People can use the VPN for free if they agree to receive ads and share their devices for other users to connect. They can also pay to remove ads and stop sharing to others. The payment can be done with fiat (normal rate) or VPNC (discount rate). Users can gain VPNC by sharing their devices for others to connect, they can also sell it on exchanges for others to buy. There will be part of VPNC reserved for developers, as the demand for VPNC increases, the price is likely to rise. Which will be one of main sources of income together with ads revenue.As each user is a single server, the VPN network is decentralised and the capability can increase through the increase of users. Which means the project does not require upgrading servers, and could effectively make sure users’ security. Besides, the rewards of VPNC may also attract people to share their devices for users to connect.

Tools and Technologies

To launch the project, we need a software to automatically check mailbox, get wallet address and send coin with download address for the VPN software. This software needs to be secure and steady, as which is the main source for people to download the VPN software. We also need several servers in different countries for users to connect, when there is not enough people to share their devices. Finally, we need both software and hardware to protect servers from attack, which may come from both dictators and competitors.

Skills Required

There needs someone who knows blockchain Technology to create the crypto currency of VPNC, which is the core of the project. Besides, someone needs to construct the VPN sites sharing networks to connect users and sharers. Then, someone must find a way to advertise the project to increase users. Finally, someone needs to make sure the security of the project.

Outcome

Once the project succeeds, people in authoritarian countries will no longer be restricted on the Internet, which may significantly enhance the democratic movement in such countries and make governments hard to brainwash people. Besides, people in different parts of the world could communicate freely, which may effectively solve problems caused by isolation and misunderstandings.
